## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Free and open source" icon="heart">
		QPlayer is free now and forever, thanks to it's permissive MIT license, 
		you are free to use and adapt QPlayer however you like!
	</Card>
	<Card title="Many formats supported" icon="puzzle">
		Supports playback of many common file formats, including: WAV, MP3, etc...
	</Card>
	<Card title="Intuitive cue stack" icon="list-format">
		Cues containing media files, stop cues, fade cues, and more, are all added to one cue stack 
		controlled by a single GO button. Multiple cues can be fired at once, cues can be paused, 
		preloaded, and more.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Fades & delays" icon="setting">
		Cues can have fade in and out times, start delays, and more to precisly craft your sound scape.
	</Card>
	<Card title="OSC integration" icon="external">
		Control QPlayer from your favourite software, with simple to integrate OSC messages.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Show tested" icon="approve-check">
		Reliability is extremely important to a good show! QPlayer, has been used and abused in 
		a real theatre setting to ensure it's reliability.
	</Card>
	<Card title="And much more" icon="sun">
		QPlayer is in active development, with many exciting features on the horizon.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
